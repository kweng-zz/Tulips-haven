The Tulip Story

🌍

Origins

Native to Central Asia, the tulip found its most famous home in the Netherlands—carried across continents by wonder and trade.

🎨

Colours

Red, yellow, pink, white, purple—even multicoloured. Each hue a story, each bloom a sentiment waiting to unfold.

🌱

Growing

Planted in autumn, basking in full or partial sun, thriving in well-drained soil—then blooming triumphantly every spring.
